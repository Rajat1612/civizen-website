How We Work

Disciplined steps that keep projects predictable and compliant.

  1. 01 — Assess Requirements

    Analyse scope, approvals, and technical standards to define the right delivery approach.

  2. 02 — Plan & Document

    Develop DPRs, tender packages, and baseline schedules with measurable checkpoints.

  3. 03 — Execute & Supervise

    Implement QA/QC plans, contractor coordination, and stakeholder reporting on-site.

  4. 04 — Audit & Optimise

    Track performance, resolve deviations, and verify compliance with contract obligations.

  5. 05 — Close & Support

    Compile handover documentation, train operators, and manage defect liability actions.
